2. Target length per subsection: 3-5 PDF pages minimum unless explicitly approved otherwise by line-by-line review.
3. For each word-group in the subsection head, include an explicit conceptual narrative block:
   - definition of the term in EM context,
   - why that term matters for angular electromagnetic structure,
   - how that term links to subsection objective and forward chain.
4. Include major equations only for governing relations and derivation milestones; define every symbol at first use.
5. For each major equation block, explain:
   - term-level mathematical role,
   - whole-equation mathematical function,
   - physical consequence and measurement implication.
6. Add compact schematic figure(s) whenever geometric or operator readability improves; include label, caption, and in-body explanation.
7. Maintain no recursion: prior established results are inherited by `\ref{...}` with no re-derivation outside HOME scope.
8. Close each subsection with a unique technical closure; subsection forward-link bridge text is optional and not mandatory.
