# Title suggestion: Volume I - Foundations, Operators, and Radiation Genesis

Selected chapters for proposed first volume: Chapters 1 to 5.

Rationale:
This split produces a complete and publishable first arc in the style of classical monographs:
first principles and invariants (Chapter 1), operator and Green-function machinery (Chapter 2),
representation and eigenfield theory (Chapter 3), realizability and finite-dimensional limits
(Chapter 4), and canonical radiation mechanisms as the first full physical closure (Chapter 5).
The reader exits Volume I with theorem-level foundations and first concrete radiative synthesis
before moving into implementation-heavy structures in subsequent volumes.
